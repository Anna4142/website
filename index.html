Anushka Deshpande

I'm a Research AI Engineer at Arcee AI, where I work on post-training research for large language models using reinforcement learning and supervised fine-tuning techniques.

I hold an MSc in Applied Computing (AI Track) from the University of Toronto (Sep 2024 -- Dec 2025). I completed my MSc in Computational Neuroscience at the Technion -- Israel Institute of Technology (2022 -- 2024), where I was advised by Dr. Sarah Keren and Dr. Ben Engelhard. My thesis, Exploring Computational and Behavioral Dynamics in Social Decision-Making via RL, is available here. I completed my BSc in Electrical Engineering at Tel Aviv University (2018 -- 2022).

My research focuses on reinforcement learning, multi-agent systems, and LLM post-training. I'm passionate about understanding decision-making in both biological and artificial systems, and I've worked on projects spanning computational neuroscience, social decision-making, and large language models.
